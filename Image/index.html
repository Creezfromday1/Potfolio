My projects

Digital core to well log tie


This graphic/digital core was created using inkscape software. The samples were that of the Leonard brother's core drilled from the Strawn formation (Pennsylvanian) in the Central Basin Platform. The digital core was created to visualize the lithologies,structures,and parasequence stacking patterns of the formation. Depth shift adjustments were made to match the digital core to the well log to interpret the regional geology and depositional history of the basin.

Wheeler diagram


The wheeler diagram created from the seismic reflector data was used to visualize the sequence stratigraphic patterns to reconstruct past sea level changes.
